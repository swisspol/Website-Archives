Background :

According to the legends, mankind once lived happily, in peace and prosperity. The rare remnants of a flourishing and technologically advanced civilization kept those legends alive.
Our history begins with war, chaos and devastation. In those ancient times men suddenly became insanely destructive, annihilating entire continents and leaving only the Unique Continent. Our ancestors had to work the barren soil with their bare hands in order to render it as fertile as we now know it. They were helped by Coltrane, a half-God. He taught them to cultivate and produce. He taught
them about construction and so they built a new City, our Capital. He taught them about warfare and gave them Robot Fighters. In gratitude, our ancestor made Coltrane King, and our sovereign today is his direct descendant. Thanks to the Coltrane dynasty, our world once again prospered.
Unfortunately, chaos seems to be taking over now. Belligerent and pitiless men, savages, are spreading terror throughout the Kingdom. Their aim is clear : to break the Monarchy to which we owe a lot and to reestablish the prehistoric system, which means the ruin of humanity.
These savages won't let anything stand in their way. The numerous victims talk about cruelty and violence. The atrocities committed are intolerable. Faced with such a peril, our glorious King Költran XII has declared a state of emergency.
The Royal Armies are ready to fight. Every citizen must feel concerned and help in any way he can. Freedom and Justice are at stake.
