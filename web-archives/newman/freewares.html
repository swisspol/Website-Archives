ReBirth Sound Editor 1.0 If you're a Techno fan, you have probably bought ReBirth RB-338, this cool program from PropellerHead Software. With ReBirth Sound Editor, you will be able to extract the TR-808 sounds used in the program. You can even replace them by your own ones!
This archive also includes a pack of drum sounds from classic techno drum expanders: 606 & 808 all recorded in CD quality (16 Bits 44.1KHz mono). You can use them to replace the drum sounds of ReBirth RB-338.		 240K
Vu-Mac 1.0 Vu-Mac diplays a stereo Vu-Meter on your screen, that will help you setting the gain of the audio source connected to your Mac. This utility is very useful for musicians. 27K
Spectro 1.1 Spectro is a high-speed audio analyze tool for Power Macs. It provides an oscilloscope and a spectrum analyzer. 33K
Sound Info 1.2 Sound Info is a simple utility which provides you many informations about the audio capabilities of your Mac and about the Sound Manager. 35K
Rhythm Factory 0.9 Build rhythms with this little program. Provided with a few sounds. 743K

Video Toaster 1.1 Video Toaster is a program which shows a video monitor on the screen (it shows in a window the video picture that's arriving to the video input). Its advantages, compared to the Appleª provided program, are:
  • it doesn't require any system extension to work.
  • it is really compatibles with the PAL / SECAM formats (theses are shown in a 768 X 576 window, not in a 640 X 480 window).
  • it allows the user to specify the audio and video compressions to use when recording a movie.
21K
Movie Machine 1.0 MovieMachine is a small utility that allows you to:
  • recompress a QuickTime movie,
  • turn a dependent movie into a self-contained movie,
  • extract tracks of the movie (like the soundtrack).
23K
