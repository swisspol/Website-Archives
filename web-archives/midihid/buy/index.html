Buy Online
Product Price
Licenses for MidiHID are sold through Kagi, a secure online store, that accepts all major credit cards, as well as PayPal or wire transfers.

The price per-user license (including all 1.x updates for free) is USD $24.
 
Buying and activating MidiHID
To get a fully functional version of MidiHID, follow these 3 simple steps:
  1. Go to the MidiHID product page at the Kagi online store and buy one or more licenses of MidiHID
  2. Once payment has been confirmed, you will receive a confirmation email that contains your registration code(s)
  3. Download the latest version of MidiHID, launch it and click on "Enter Registration Code" at the bottom of the main window.
If you have questions regarding your order, simply visit the Kagi Order Tracking page.
