WaterRace is shipping!

WaterRace is an offshore racing game with unique characters, levels and ships, each having their own technical characteristics. Mastering the different ships and levels is an extraordinary challenge.
The beauty and the diversity of the circuits located in different parts of the world, and the incredible soundtrack create a thrilling atmosphere.

> Who did this game:

WaterRace was developed using Macs and for the Mac by an brand new and independent team of european developers called "French Touch". They worked during 2 years on this game for free and during their free time, to bring you the best possible and original game for your Mac. The result is finally here, and we hope you will enjoy it!

> WaterRace is available on CD and contains:

 • music in the audio tracks of the CD
• English, French and German versions of WaterRace
• printed users manual in English, French and German

> The full game features:

• 9 levels with detailed and animated environments
(having each an easy, a medium and a difficult track to race on)
• 9 high speed boats with unique characteristics and driving styles
• 9 real-time animated 3D characters in the interface of the game
• compete against challenging AI opponents
• accelerated 3D engine with high resolution graphics
• impressive soundtrack
• LAN network play up to 8 players over TCP/IP
• 3D editing tools to create your own ships and levels
... and of course, a "Mac touch".

> Minimum recommended system:

Power Macintosh G3/G4, PowerBook G3, iMac, iBook with at least 6Mb VRAM
MacOS 8.5 or later (MacOS 9.0 prefered)
100MB of free hard disk space
Optimized for the latest 3D accelerator cards: ATI Rage 128, ATI Radeon, 3Dfx Voodoo4, 3Dfx Voodoo5, Formac Proformance III+

WaterRace is the only full 3D game from the last 2 years that runs perfectly on iMacs rev B and higher & all iBooks!!
