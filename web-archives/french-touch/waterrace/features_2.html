WaterRace levels

All WaterRace levels are very unique: this means day and night levels, inside and outside levels, complex or simple tracks, etc... Each level has its own specifities and has to be played differently.

The levels of WaterRace also differ from the levels of any other racing game (with water or not):
It's essentially due to their conception: usually racing games level designers first create the track of the level, then start building a decor around it. And you end up always being inside of a «tunnel » when playing the level.
On the contrary, the WaterRace level designers are working in a completely different way: they first create a complete world with a special atmosphere, unique 3D models, lighting effects, ambient sounds... And thereafter only create the racing tracks
inside this world.
The main consequence is that WaterRace levels do not look like typical computer game levels: all the WaterRace levels are extremely riched, detailed with lots of 3D models, strong colors, unique ambiance... and plenty of animation on them. Let's not forget the audio environnement: each level spot many sound fx that create a rich sound atmosphere on top of a unique high-quality music.

The player is also totally free in WaterRace levels: he does not have to stay on the track; he can just spend time visiting the level, discove hidden places, shortcuts...

Each level offers three really different tracks related to each difficulty level, that go through different places of the world.While the difficulty level increases, the tracks become longer and more difficult: hard turns, narrow ways...

So nine levels in WaterRace does not means 9 races to complete, but 27 different ones!
