For immediate publication

 
    WaterRace add-ons
    Lausanne, Switzerland
    March 1st, 2001


    French Touch has released 6 exclusive and free add-ons for its arcade racing
    game WaterRace. They demonstrate that the game has a very open architecture
    and since it is provided with complete editing tools, it's easy to create
    totally weird add-ons!

    The add-ons currently available feature:
    • an alien space ship,
    • a mad cow,
    • a star wars racer,
    • and more...

    You can download them all from our web site and install them with a simple
    drag & drop:
    http://www.french-touch.net/waterrace/addons.cfm

    ** note to webmasters: do not mirror the files, since we might update them
    frequently **

    Owner of the complete version of WaterRace, don't forget to download and
    install the WaterRace 1.0.1 update which fixes some gameplay and technical
    issues:
    -> http://www.french-touch.net/support/updates.cfm

    ** note to webmasters: do not mirror the files, since they will become
    outdated soon **


    We will also release a patch, probably next week, to fix a problem some
    users have been reporting regarding the fact WaterRace will not recognize
    that the CD is in the drive.


    Remember you can buy the Waterrace CD (with printed users manual) for US $39
    only (international shipping & handling included!!) from our web site:
    -> http://www.french-touch.net/waterrace/order.cfm

    WaterRace is also available in stores in Germany, Austria and Switzerland
    (distribution by Arktis) and in a few weeks in the rest of Europe
    (distribution by Softline).




    Sincerely,

    The French Touch team
French Touch is a independent European game developer dedicated to the creation of exclusive 3D games for the Macintosh. Their first title is a water racing game called WaterRace.
