Terrain Builder screenshots gallery :

These are some screenshots of Terrain Builder that show you some features of this powerful tool. These images were made while editing the Jamaica level of WaterRace.
Click on a screenshot image to see it full screen.

 

 
The interface of Terrain Builder which is very similar to the interface of Infinity Model Builder, the 3D model editor from French Touch.
The Terrain Builder workspace contains a construction window, an inspector palette, a sequencer and as many preview windows as you wish - everything with 3D real-time rendering of course!

 

 

 

People who love low-level 3D editing will be satisfied with Terrain Builder powerful mesh editing features - also completely in real-time.

 

 

 

 

 

 The sequencer which includes motion paths, real-time editing and many utility fonctions.
You can even create fly-throughs of your level!

 

 

 

 

 

 With Terrain Builder, you can place 3D models (made with Infinity Model Builder) on your terrain in a few seconds just by clicking with the mouse.
It's really the fastest and easiest way to create levels.

 

 

 

 

Terrain Builder even include powerful ambient sound placement also with the mouse.
You can set volume, distance, pitch and more for each sound source. You can even listen to the sound environnement while in Terrain Builder.

 

 

 

 

 

 Create tracks and place bonus items for WaterRace levels with a single click!

 

 

 

 

 

 Terrain Builder also includes gorgeous special FX like rain or snow you can preview in real-time.
