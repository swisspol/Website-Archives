Emily, the first 3D animated bunny for your desktop

 

 

What is Emily :

The Emily application is French Touch's new years 3D animated card (with music!).

Basically, Emily is a auto-executable application based on the Infinity 3D Engine.

 

System requirements :

To be able to use Emily, you need:
• a PowerMacintosh - a G3 (all G3 Power Macintoshes have ATI 3D chips on board) or a PowerMacintosh with a 3D accelerator card is highly recommended.
• System 7.1 or later.
• QuickDraw 3D Rave.
