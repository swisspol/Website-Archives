WaterRace Pilots :

When the player starts a brand new game, the first step before participating to the WaterRace championship is to create a pilot:

The player is offered a choice among eight characters, each of them having it own life and characteristics, and of course a unique racing engine. They all have the official title of "WaterRace champion" on their own terrain.

All the pilots will be present in the interface along the championship as real-time 3D animated characters. Before the races and after the races, watch them commenting the results, making fun of you if you lost the race or congratulating you!


Discover the profiles of three out of the eight WaterRace champions (we will not reveal all the characters to the future player):



Name Leah
Age 24
Terrain Special
Ship G-Boat
Leah is the great enigmatic star of the international championship.

The only thing we know about her is her performances: she hasn't lost any race for many years.

This charismatic character lives somewhere in France on the sea side where she trains in secret on the boat she developped herself: the G-Boat.

SHE CANNOT BE SELECTED BY THE PLAYER.


Name Francesca Toucci
Age 26
Terrain Venice (Italy)
Ship Formula 1
The Italian girl combines speediness and agility, both qualities she acquired while training in her native city of Venice.

Francesca developed a passion for piloting while she was driving «vaporetos» to pay for her studies in art history. Today, she keeps a deep interest in art,but she organizes her life around the Grand Prix tournaments.

Being alert and independent makes her an exceptional pilot. An expert in Formula 1 in shore, she does not leave much chance to her opponents.


Name Sarah Craft
Age 32
Terrain Army Base (USA)
Ship Drag Boat
Don ’t trust appearances! Sergeant Instructor Sarah Craft was led off from the Marine Corps on charge of having striken and severely wounded a high officer. She was then testing the «Sea Fighter »project, the present Drag Boat.

The aggressive temper of Sarah pushed her to the top of the motonautical competition. She is the unrivaled pilot of the fabulous Drag Boat.

Thanks to her former relationships, she organizes today a WaterRace contest in a U,S, Navy base.

Watch out !: with her Drag Boat and in her own site, she is a real «fighter»!


Name Wang Lee
Age 28
Terrain Halong Bay (VietNam)
Ship Hydroplane
This is Number One WaterRace champion in Asia

Wang was born in Hong-Kong,in the floating village. However, it is in the Halong Bay that he got his qualification. He really knows the place like his pocket.

His favorite boat is the mighty Hydroplane, which requires art and practice to maneuver.
This means that Wang is a terrific competitor on his site as well as everywhere.
