WaterRace levels :

WaterRace is played on nine closed circuits located in different parts of the world. Each circuit has been specifically designed to depict a geographical and cultural environment, dramatized by sound and light.

All levels are very unique: this means day and night levels, inside and outside levels, etc... Like the ships, each level has its own specifities and has to be played differently.


Why do the levels of WaterRace differ from the levels of any other racing game (with water or not)?

Primary because of their conception: usually racing games level designers first create the track of the level then start building a decor around it. And you end up always being inside of a "tunnel" when playing the level.
The WaterRace level designers are working completely differently: they first create a complete world with a special athmosphere, unique 3D models, lighting effects... And thereafter only they create the racing tracks inside this world.

The main consequence for the player is that he his really free to go whereever he wants: he does not have to stay on the track like in most racing games. He can just spend time visiting the level, discove hidden places, shortcuts... The player can simply explore the area.

That's why there is this "open feeling" in the game, especially in levels like Jamaica.


Level of difficulty:

Also in this area, WaterRace is very different from other racing games. Usually changing difficulty level means changing the way your opponents drive, increasing the difficulty to control the racing engines or adding more and more obstacles on the track... but you're still racing in the same circuits.

In WaterRace, the difficulty influences the tracks of the races: each circuit offers three really different tracks related to each difficulty levels, that go through different places of the world. While the difficulty level increases, the tracks become longer and more difficult: hard turns, narrow ways...

So nine levels in WaterRace does not means 9 races to complete, but 27 different ones!

Scotland level :

The decor is a Scottish lake,whose surface is swept by an icy wind (which carries Celtic tunes). A rising storm darkens the sky. On the shore of the lake, an old castle overlooks the surrounding hills, where sheep graze peacefully. In the middle of the lake comes out an island, where men of the Paleolithic era have erected stones.
The racing circuit has been drafted through this magical world. An ideal place where the WaterRace pilots can set free their speed impulse. But beware of the rocks...and of the lake monster!










Weather conditions Foggy
Time Middle of the day
 
Tracks length 600m -> 900m
 
Wave height 0.25m
Stream 0.025m/s

 

Venice level :

Sun rises on Venice, still asleep. In the dawn light,gondolas are waiting for honey-mooners. But these are abruptly awaken by the roaring of the «Formula 1 in shore» warming up for the Venician Grand Prix of the WaterRace season.
The circuit goes through the canals of the well known city, through the harbor, and through unusual plazas and
popular districts.
It's a very short circuit, but probably the most difficult one: piloting requires full attention: sharp angle curves, narrow ways forbidding passing, and various obstacles (stakes,gondolas,motor boats …).









Weather conditions Cloudy sky

Jamaica level :

In a paradise lagoon,the ships race between mountainous islands and beaches covered in palm trees, coral reefs, and wrecked galleons full of treasures. The sky is deep blue, the sea is quiet and still. A stiff breeze carries to our ears tunes of reggae, which adds to the appeal of the place. Here and there, a shark boldly strokes the boats with his fins while shoals of small fishes swim along.
The place looks like heaven,but beware,your opponents are no tourists!










Weather conditions Clear sky
Time Middle of the day
 
Tracks length 680m -> 1000m
 
Wave height 0.2m
Stream 0.06m/s

 

Army Base level :

This level is the only night level of WaterRace.
The site represents a deep-sea navy harbor, where are stationed a plane-carrier, a destroyer, smaller navy units, and two submarines (located within a concrete shed). The circuit runs between the ships, within the submarine base, and along barracks protected by tanks and guns.
The design of the race is pretty simple, but it's quite a difficult level of the game because of the strong turns and bad visibility. Furthermore, you have to avoid the many pillars of the numerous pontoons while driving at speed greater than 150Km/h.










Weather conditions Full moon
Time Middle of the night
 
Tracks length 500m -> 800m
 
Wave height 0.15m
