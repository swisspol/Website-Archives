Welcome to the online press room

This section is here for magazines and web site editors

You will find here:

  • the archives of our press releases,
  • press kits which are packs that contain hi-resolution versions of our logos (around 2500x1000 pixels), text information & exclusive screenshots,
  • low-resolution versions of our logos to be used on web sites (Direct media),
  • ready to print versions of our logos in TIFF CMYK 300dpi,
  • contact information and links to web pages that provide more information.
