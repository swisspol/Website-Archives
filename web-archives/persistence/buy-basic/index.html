Persistence Basic - USD $34

Persistence Basic provides most of the great features of Persistence for a cheaper price, but with a few limitations compared to the Pro version.

Licenses for Persistence Basic are sold through Kagi, a secure online store, that accepts all major credit cards, as well as PayPal or wire transfers.

This version is sufficient if you already have access to online storage (like an FTP server or Apple iDisk), and only need to do simple backups.
Product limitations compared to Persistence Pro:
  • Total amount of backed up data is limited to 25 Gb
  • Backups to external drives are not available
  • Backups to Amazon S3 or SFTP servers are not available
  • Processing and transfer of multiple backup data files simultaneously is not available
  • Running an application, AppleScript or shell script before or after the backups is not available
  • The advanced interface to build complex files and directories exclusion rules is not available (only the simple interface is available)

Product price:
  • Per-user license (includes all 1.x updates for free): USD $34
  • Educational, non-profit and bulk discounts are available: see details on the product page at Kagi

Buying and activating Persistence Basic:
  1. Go to the Persistence product page at the Kagi online store and buy one or more licenses of Persistence Basic
  2. Once payment has been confirmed, you will receive a confirmation email that contains your registration code(s)
  3. Download the latest version of Persistence Basic using the link on the right side of the page, launch it and click on "Enter Registration Code" at the bottom of the main window.

If you have questions regarding your order, simply visit the Kagi Order Tracking page.
